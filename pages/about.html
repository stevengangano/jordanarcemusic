About Jordan Arce

I have been playing music since I was five years old and have never stopped since. I have been performing for ten years. I’m originally from the Bay Area, but moved to Modesto, CA and San Diego, CA for school, and have lived in Mississippi, Arkansas, and Nevada. I am a man of many passions who strives to make a life worth living by indulging in them. I am an avid photographer (visit my instagram at i.jordanphotography), and consumer of movies and shows (check out my movie reviews at www.letterboxd.com/indiana_jordan). I am also a full on gear head, and would love to feature your car in my YouTube car show “Middle Gear” (check out the Middle Gear channel!) I love science and learning about nature and space, and so feel that much more connected to animals and pets. I play the piano, guitar, and bass, and am deeply rooted in blues, rock, funk, and r&b. Billy Joel, Lionel Richie, Stevie Ray Vaughan, The Beatles, and John Mayer are just a few of the many influences that have shaped my passion for music. Please follow me on SoundCloud and YouTube, and visit this website for updates. Contact me if you enjoy my music and would like a one hour set at your venue featuring originals, and some good old fashioned blues!"
