About Jordan Arce

My name is Jordan Arce and I have been performing for 10 years. I play the guitar, piano, and bass. I'm originally from San Francisco, but grew up in San Diego and recently moved back to the Bay Area to pursue my graduate degree. I am currently a biology graduate student and teacher at San Francisco State University. I enjoy the outdoors, driving cars, watching movies, skateboarding, and playing with my cat. I also enjoy walking dogs, so if anyone never needs me to take care of their dog, please hit me up. I enjoy playing blues and jazz and John Mayer has been my biggest inspiration as musician. I have performed for numerous bands and collaborated with artists throughout California. I'm open to performing shows throughout the Bay Area, so please shoot me a message! For samples of my music, checkout my soundcloud, spotify, and youtube on my page!
